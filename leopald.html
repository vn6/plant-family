Leopald

Leopald is the only plant that lives on a different table than the rest. He is a babytears plant that lives on a small table near a big window, in order to get more light. Leopald's pot is above a plastic lid and paper towel that acts as a water plate for the drainage. He has grown enough for his vines to hang off the table!
